CSS Day

@CSSDayConf 2016

Event
2016-06-16T17:43:00Z

Continental Europe and web development conferences? Well, Amsterdam is the place to be, think no further than CSS Day.

Day 1

In 2016—for the first time—the actual CSS Day on June 17 was preceded by an HTML Special, with the following amazing speakers.

Talks & Speakers

  • “<a>” by Jeremy Keith
  • “<link>” by Yoav Weiss
  • “<layer>” by Peter-Paul Koch
  • “<source>” by Simon Pieters
  • “<noscript>” by Niels Leenheer
  • “<input>” by Monica Dinculescu
  • “<iframe>” by Peter van der Zee
  • “<html>” by Lea Verou

In case you’ve noticed that all talk titles are HTML elements, it didn’t stop there. Not even the coffee <br>eaks were safe. 😉

Day 2

Unlike the HTML Special the day before, the focus on the actual CSS Day was on—wait for it—CSS. And the speakers did not disappoint #MindBlown.

Talks & Speakers

  • “CSS for Engineers and Devs” by Harry Roberts
  • “CSS and Accessibility” by Léonie Watson
  • “Going Mediaqueryless” by Vasilis van Gemert
  • “CSS and Email” by Mark Robbins
  • “CSS and SVG” by Amelia Bellamy-Royds
  • “CSS4 Color” by Chris Lilley
  • “Layout” by Greg Whitworth
  • “Blend Modes” by Una Kravets
