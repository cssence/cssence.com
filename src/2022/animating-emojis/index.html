Time flies, but at least it didn’t take me another twelve months to come up with my next emoji animation.

There are a lot of clock emojis, and the clock faces are perfect candidates to do something with them. Unlike all previous adventures in this series, where I’ve had two emojis to alternate between, this time there is no cross-fade. Instead, twelve emojis are shown in rapid succession.

This would also make a nice loading spinner. See for yourself.
